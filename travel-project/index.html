Choose Your Destination

Have you ever wanted to go on a magical adventure? Meet some exciting new friends? Perhaps even discover who you truly are? You're in luck! There are two amazing places to choose from. Down in Orlando, Florida there are two amazing theme parks that can give you the escape from reality expierence.

No matter the choice you make, these two destinations can be all inclusive. Each have these great benefits:

  • On-Site Resorts
  • Several Fine Dining Choices
  • Rides for all Ages
  • Shows and Events
  • And So Much More...

Either choice will be an unforgettable expierence, however everyone always has a different preference.This is where your decision comes into play. Below are the descriptions to each of the theme parks. Lets decide which vacation is the best for you!

Universal Studios

Have you ever wondered which house at Hogwarts you belong to? Save the world from Decepticons? Explore inside a Dr. Seuss book? Look no further! Universal Studios is right for you! Inside this theme park, it is packing with all your favorite movies and make you feel part of the action. Come check out Univeresal Studios!

Disney World

Magic does exist and dreams can come true! Become your very own Disney princess and meet them all! Sail the Seven Seas with Jack Sparrow or even discover the world of Avatar. All of this is available at Disney World. You will never fly home to the second star on the right after spending a day here! Come check out Disney World!
