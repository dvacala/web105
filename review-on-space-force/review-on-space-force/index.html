Review on Space Force

Summary

Space Force is a new tv show that hit Netflix Friday, May 28th. The show follows Steve Carell's character, General Mark R. Naird, as he struggles to be the first Chief of Space Operations. This show is a workplace comedy and shows many fun and unique challenges this underdog military branch faces.

General Naird dedicated his entire life to the U.S. Airforce and it came first to the eyes of his wife and daughter. During the show you see him realize how much of his life was missed with his family within his new role. You also see a budding friendship between many of the characters throughout the show.

My Review

I personally thought the show did very well at capturing subtle comedy. This kind of comedy is where the joke is not highlighted and is flowed with what the actors do the background of each scene. It is also shown in some of the hilarious things characters say as are serious in what they say. One of my favorite is a quote from Naird, Cheap generals are like cheap enchiladas - you end up paying for it on the back end.

The main plot of the show was capturing to myself as the audience. This kind of humor reminds me a lot of The Office. This should be at no surprise since Steve Carell also played a huge role in that show as well. The reviews from others were negative, however I feel this is kind of show has a very specific targeted audience.
