Commander Format Rules

In Each Deck

  • 1 commander card
  • 99 other cards
  • Only one copy of each card, unless it is a basic land
  • All the cards have to follow the color identity of the commander

The Game

  • Can have 2-6 players
  • Each player starts with 40 life
  • Each commander starts in the command zone
  • Before play, each player rolls a 6 sided die, players play on order for whoever rolled highest number to lowest
  • Last player remaining, wins.

    • Color Identity

    The color identity is of the deck is determined by the colors of the commander. The identity can come from any part of the card. This includes the casting cost as well as any mana symbols in the text. Colorless cards are allowed in any deck. To find more about the types of mana and their symbols, please click here.

    Command Zone

    The command zone is where the commander resides when it is not in play. The commander should be in the command zone at the beginning of every game. The first time the commander is cast from the command zone, it is the normal cost. If your commander is put into your hand, graveyard, or exile from anywhere, have the option of returning it to the command zone. When you recast your commander from the command zone after the first time, there is a commander tax of two additional colorless mana for each previous time it has been cast from the command zone each game.

    Commander Damage

    Each time a player is dealt damage by the commander, this is called commander damage. If a player has been dealt 21 or more damage by the same commander over the course of a single game, the player loses the game. If you are dealt 5 damage from opponet A's commander and 3 damage from opponet B's commander, then you would keep track of them seperately. It would not combine and equal 8 commander damage.

    Banned Cards

    In each format of Magic the Gathering, there are banned cards. These are not allowed to be in any commander decks. For a complete list of banned cards, please click here.
